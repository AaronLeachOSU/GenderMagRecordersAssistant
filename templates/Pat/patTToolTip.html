Pat's Attitudes Towards Tinkering
Pat will tinker and explore software features a bit, but if they don't think that they are getting closer to her goal, they will revert to familiar ways. When Pat sees a need to learn new technology, they do so by trying out new features or commands to see what they do and to understand how the software works. When they do this, they do so purposefully; that is, they reflect on each bit of feedback they get along the way to understand how the feature might benefit them. Eventually, if they don't think it will get them closer to what they want to achieve, they will revert back to ways that they already know will work.

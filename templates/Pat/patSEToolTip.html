Pat's Computer Self-Efficacy
Pat's medium computer self efficacy means that they will persist through some challenges when using software for a reasonable amount of time. Pat has medium computer self-efficacy, meaning that they have some self-confidence in performing computing tasks other than the ones they are familiar with. This has a variety of impacts on how they use software. For example,they will keep on trying to figure out how to achieve what they have set out to do for quite awhile; they don't give up right away when computers or technology present a challenge to them.
